Executive Summary 2


Background
Understanding demographic factors influencing suicide rates is crucial for effective mental health strategies. This project uses data analysis to examine how age, gender, and socio-economic status impact suicide trends. By applying predictive modeling to Kaggle-sourced data, we aim to provide insights that help develop targeted interventions and improve global mental health outcomes.



Problem Statement
How do demographic factors affect suicide rates across different regions? Despite a 9% reduction in suicide rates over the past years, significant variations remain, particularly among males and those aged 35-54. This project investigates how factors like age, gender, and socio-economic status interact to influence suicide rates. By applying advanced data analysis techniques, including predictive modeling, we aim to uncover key drivers of these variations and provide insights to develop targeted mental health interventions.


Objectives
The objective is to create an interactive Power BI dashboard that visualizes geographical variations in suicide rates. This dashboard will highlight key demographic factors and trends, allowing users to identify regions with higher or lower rates. By providing insights into how age, gender, and socio-economic factors influence suicide rates across different areas, the dashboard will support mental health professionals and policymakers in developing targeted interventions and strategies.
