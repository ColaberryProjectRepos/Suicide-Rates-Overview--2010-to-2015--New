Project Overview:
The escalating issue of suicide presents a profound challenge in global mental health, with 7 million suicides recorded between 2010 and 2015. Despite a promising 9% year-over-year reduction in suicides, the persistently high rates among males and individuals aged 35-54 highlight the urgent need for targeted interventions. This project aims to delve into the complex demographic factors influencing suicide rates, enabling a deeper understanding of the underlying causes. By analyzing these critical elements, we strive to develop strategies that effectively address and mitigate the risk factors contributing to this global crisis.
