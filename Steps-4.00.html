Data Preparation
To prepare the data for analysis, we cleaned it by addressing missing values, handling outliers, and correcting inconsistencies to ensure its accuracy and reliability. Additionally, we conducted transformations like normalization and categorical variable encoding to make the data suitable for analysis.


Model Selection
We selected the Random Forest model for analyzing suicide rate variations due to its capability to handle complex interactions and variability in demographic data. Its ensemble learning approach ensures robust performance and provides clear insights into how demographic and socio-economic factors influence suicide rates across different regions.



1. Accuracy (90.7%):
Definition: Accuracy measures the overall correctness of the model's predictions. It is the ratio of correctly predicted instances to the total instances.
Interpretation: In this case, the model correctly predicted the target variable with an accuracy of 90.7% during cross-validation. It indicates a high level of overall correctness in the model's predictions.
2. F1 Score (90.8%):
Definition: The F1 score is the harmonic mean of Precision and Recall. It provides a balance between precision (correct positive predictions) and recall (correctly identifying actual positives).
Interpretation: A high F1 score (90.8%) suggests that the model has a good balance between making accurate positive predictions and correctly identifying all actual positive instances.
3. Precision (90.8%):
Definition: Precision measures the accuracy of positive predictions. It is the ratio of true positive predictions to the total positive predictions made by the model.
Interpretation: The model achieved a precision of 90.8%, indicating that when it predicts a positive outcome, it is correct 90.8% of the time.
4. Recall (90.7%):
Definition: Recall, also known as sensitivity or true positive rate, measures the model's ability to identify all relevant instances. It is the ratio of true positive predictions to the total actual positive instances.
Interpretation: A recall of 90.7% indicates that the model captured 90.7% of all actual positive instances in the dataset.
