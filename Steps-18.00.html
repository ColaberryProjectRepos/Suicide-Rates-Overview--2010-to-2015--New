Creating and Formatting Waterfall the Chart 18

1. Select the chart type to "Waterfall."
2. Next drag in the "Measure Name" from our "Categories" table into the 'Category" section of the chart. Then drag in the "Display" measure to the "Values" section of the chart. (Make sure to change the "Display" measure to percent values)
3. Change the green color on the chart to our "Main Blue" color and keep the red.
4. Remove the "Total" value from the chart and make the "Y-Axis" start at 0.
